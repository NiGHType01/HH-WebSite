After School Club

Opening Hours 3pm - 6pm
(£8 per child)

Children are collected from their schools when they finish and
brought back to the club. Children that attend extra curricular activities
(football, netball, gym etc. can also be picked up if arranged)

Children have full access to all our resources, Toys, Board Games,
Xbox Kinect, Pool Tables, Laptops etc. Along with full access to
the outdoor area, outdoor Toys and Bikes (weather permitting).

A snack/tea is offered at no extra charge, from our daily menu...
(spaghetti bolognese, wraps, beans on toast, curry & rice etc.),
please see menu for details.

Children are always encouraged to help themselves to
fresh fruit and drinks, that we make available at all times.

Any messages from school will be passed on to parents via our staff
