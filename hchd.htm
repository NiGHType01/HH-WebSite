Holiday Club (Half Day)

Opening Hours 9am - 3:30pm
(£15 per child)

Bookings for the holiday club must be made in advance on
the appropriate form. This must be handed in before the deadline date.

The Holiday Club can only run if there is enough demand and there is
sufficient income to cover the running costs. A decision is made as soon
as possible after the deadline date in order to let people make
alternative arrangements if necessary.

Children have full access to all our resources, Toys, Board Games,
Xbox Kinect, Pool Tables, Laptops etc. Along with full access to the
outdoor area, outdoor Toys and Bikes (weather permitting).

Children are always encouraged to help themselves to
fresh fruit and drinks, that we make available at all times.

Trips may be planned during the holidays,
Chester Zoo, Theme Parks, Cinemas etc.

These trips come at an extra cost (in some cases these are optional).
